Sick or Injured Care Plans

We are here to help your sick or injured Pet.

Seeing your Pet sick or in pain is difficult enough without stressing over a high, unexpected veterinary bill. We designed our Sick or Injured Pet plans to get your companion the treatment they need right away, with a payment structure that supports your family.

01

Transparent Pricing

We assess your Pet’s needs and provide a quote that is often 30% to 40% lower than traditional vet hospitals.

02

Half the Cost Deferred

We immediately defer 50% of the cost. This amount is only owed if you ever decide to cancel your membership.

03

Manageable Installments

You can pay the active 50% balance in 12 simple monthly payments, or all at once.

04

Focus on Healing

We treat your Pet right away so you can focus on helping them recover.
