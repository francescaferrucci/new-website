Find the Right Pet — for Life

Hannah’s Lifetime Matching Program™

A great relationship starts with the right match. At Hannah, we believe every Pet deserves the right family, and every family deserves a Pet whose personality, energy, and needs fit naturally into their life.

That’s why we created the Hannah Lifetime Matching Program™ — a thoughtful placement process designed with guidance from behavioral experts, psychologists, and veterinary professionals to help create lasting, successful bonds between Pets and Pet Parents.

1Tell us about your lifestyle
2Meet Pets that fit your family
3Receive guidance and support
4Begin life together with Hannah by your side

Hannah the Pet Society

Hannah the Pet Society helps extend Hannah’s mission beyond the hospital walls by supporting programs that strengthen the bond between Pets and people.

Through placement support, education, community programs, and charitable initiatives, the Society works to help more Pets find loving homes and remain with the families who care for them.
